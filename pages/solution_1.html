9th Grade: Setting Up Your Stack Experience

Stack creates a personalized Timeline that visually maps each freshman's academic and extracurricular journey.
Students share their interests, and Stack curates personalized opportunities that match.
Students browse a centralized hub, with Stack highlighting opportunities based on their evolving interests.
Students add opportunities to their wishlist and drag them onto their timeline to see their journey take shape.

10th Grade: Exploring Interests to Build Direction

Stack prompts reflection after each activity, helping students recall and evaluate their experiences.
Stack helps students spot patterns by organizing experiences and highlighting what they enjoy most.
Stack helps students identify interests and plan next steps by turning reflections into personalized recommendations.

11th & 12th Grade: Finding a Path Forward

Stack surfaces key moments of growth, helping them recognize patterns, set goals, and confidently share their story with teachers, counselors, and colleges.
Stack's Future Filter creates personalized pathways that show students how to apply their skills in meaningful contexts.
