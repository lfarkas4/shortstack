Introducing Stack...

A dynamic progress visualizer that helps students reflect, recognize patterns, and explore what's next.

We designed Stack to grow with students, supporting their needs at every stage of their high school journey.

✦

9th Grade: Setting Up Your Stack Experience

A personalized timeline maps academic and extracurricular milestones.

Interests unlock curated opportunities tailored to each student.

A centralized hub highlights new opportunities based on evolving interests.

Drag saved experiences onto the timeline to plan and shape your story.

✦

10th Grade: Exploring Interests to Build Direction

Prompts after each activity help students process and learn from their experiences.

Visual patterns reveal where students find the most meaning and joy.

Insights from past experiences help students plan their next step.

✦

11th & 12th Grade: Finding a Path Forward

Key moments of growth are captured to guide storytelling and goal-setting.

Future Filter shows how personal strengths map to real-world opportunities.

✦

Support System Touchpoints: Bridging the Gap

For Parents:

Automatic email updates keep families informed and engaged in the journey.

For Guidance Counselors:

Organized timelines and reflections support deeper student check-ins.

For Teachers:

Interest trends across classes highlight opportunities for cross-curricular planning.
