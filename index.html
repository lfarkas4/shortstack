Designing for Student-Centered Futures

Stack is a student-centered planning system co-designed with South Fayette to help high schoolers navigate their growth journeys. Our solution reimagines how students explore interests, make decisions, and plan meaningful next steps.

Understanding the Challenge

High schoolers face a fragmented, unclear path when it comes to planning their futures. Learn about how our project addresses this disconnect between school and real-world readiness.

Our Research Insights

Through interviews, system mapping, and workshops, we identified core themes around student decision-making, support structures, and exploration gaps. These insights directly shaped our design priorities.

Introducing Our Solution

Stack is a visual planning platform that helps students reflect on their interests, track their experiences, and map out next steps with clarity. The result? A tool that empowers students to own their growth journey.
